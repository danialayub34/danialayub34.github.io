I am starting this blog because I think better when I write. A thought held in the head stays vague; the same thought put into a sentence has to commit to something, and the act of editing — rereading, cutting, rephrasing — is where the actual thinking happens.

Most of what appears here will be about early childhood education: observation, documentation, play, and the slow work of building pedagogical practices that teams can actually sustain. Some of it will be about other things entirely.

This is a first post, and mostly a placeholder. Delete it, or keep it as a marker of where things began.
