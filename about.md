# About

I am Danial, an early childhood educator in Singapore. I work primarily with preschoolers, and I am interested in inquiry-based and play-based pedagogy, pedagogical documentation, and what it means to build practices that are structured without being burdensome.

This blog is where I think through writing. Some of it is about early childhood education; some of it is about everything else.
